My Favorite Makeup Products

Foundations

Applying foundation is a vital part of my makeup. After trying many foundation products from different brands. There are three foundation products that I love the most

YSL- All Hours Foundation

Pros: YSL All Hours Foundation is a long-lasting makeup that provides full coverage for up to 24 hours. It offers a smooth, matte finish that controls oil and minimizes shine. The lightweight formula blends easily and suits all skin types, including oily skin. It also helps keep your skin hydrated while staying in place throughout the day.

Cons: YSL- All Hours Foundation is pretty expensive

Rate: 7.5/10

ESTEE LAUDER - Double Wear Stay-in-Place Foundation

Pros: This foundation provides long-lasting coverage that stays in place for up to 24 hours. It offers a natural matte finish that controls oil and reduces shine throughout the day. The formula is lightweight and buildable, making it suitable for all skin types. It also resists sweat, humidity, and transfers, keeping your makeup intact all day.

Cons: Because of thick texture, it feels heavy on the skin. My skin is dry, so I sometimes find it hard to blend.

Rate: 7/10

NARS - Nars Light Reflecting Foundation

Pros: This foundation that gives your skin a dewy glow. It feels very light on skin. The coverage level is pretty good. The lightweight formula is infused with skincare ingredients that help improve the skin's texture over time. It also lasts well throughout the day without settling into fine lines or pores.

Cons: The shade range is limited, which may not suit all skin tones

Rate: 8.5/10

Lips

Dior Lip Glow

Pros: The balm is lightweight and smooth, making it perfect for daily wear without feeling sticky. I usually put it on before bedtime, and my lips keep hydrated. The package is beautiful and trendy. It doesn't feel heavy on lips.

Cons: the color does not last long. I need to reapply it after I drink or eat anything.

Rate: 8/10

YSL Lip Glow

Pros: I love the package of it so much since it looks so beautiful. The shade range is a big plus. It feels very light on lips. It keeps the lips hydrated for such a long time.

Cons: the product's glossy finish may not be preferred by those who prefer matte or less shiny textures on their lips.

Rate: 9/10

KIKO - 3D Hydra Lipgloss

Pros: Kiko 3D Hydra Lipgloss provides a high-shine finish that gives lips a plump, voluminous look. It is enriched with hydrating ingredients that keep lips soft and moisturized. The lightweight formula is non-sticky, making it comfortable to wear all day. Available in a range of vibrant shades, it adds a pop of color and shine to any makeup look.

Cons: the formula can be slightly greasy for those who prefer a lighter texture. The price is affordable

Rate: 7.5/10

RHODE - Lip Tint

Pros: The package is pretty and easy to use. The color shade is suitable for all skin tone

Cons: Since it is so popular, it is not easy to buy

Rate: 8/10

Maybeline

Pros: The color shade is diverse. The price is affordable. It does feel heavy on lips. The lipticks last long

Cons: The formula can be drying for some users, particularly for those with already dry lips.

Rate: 7.5/10

CHARLOTTE TILBURY - Matte Revolution

Pros: The formula is enriched with hydrating ingredients like lipstick tree extract and orchid extract, keeping lips soft and moisturized. It provides long-lasting wear, staying in place for hours without fading.

Cons: The matte finish, while comfortable, can sometimes accentuate dry patches on the lips.

Rate: 7/10
